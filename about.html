About Us

Modern Japanese Cuisine

Welcome to Kagemori, a Japanese cuisine restaurant inspired by the serene beauty of nature. The name Kagemori, meaning "Shadow Forest", reflects our vision of providing a tranquil dining experience reminiscent of a hidden woodland retreat. In the midst of life's hustle and bustle, Kagemori offers a peaceful sanctuary where you can unwind, enjoy exceptional food, and escape to a world of calm. Whether you're here to enjoy a casual meal or celebrate a special occasion, Kagemori provides a serene environment where every guest is treated with care and respect.

Our Philosophy

At Kagemori, we are dedicated to bringing you the finest Japanese cuisine, blending traditional flavors with modn techniques. We use a combination of high-quality imported ingredients from Japan and the freshest locally sourced produce to create dishes that celebrate the changing seasons. Our menu features a variety of delights, from expertly prepared sashimi and flavorful ramen to crispy tempura, each crafted to highlight the richness of our ingredients. Whether you're a longtime fan of Japanese cuisine or discovering it for the first time, Kagemori promises a memorable and satisfying dining experience.

Our Story

A Legacy in the Shadows

The story of Kagemori begins in 1914, with a small teahouse nestled deep within the forests of Kyoto. Founded by Haruto Mikage, the teahouse was a sanctuary for weary travellers seeking respite from their journeys. Surrounded by the serenity of nature, guests are able to connect with the beauty of the seasons and find peace beneath the towering trees. Haruto’s philosophy was deeply rooted in the belief that food should reflect nature’s harmony, crafted with care and served with warmth. Visitors often praised his ability to capture the forests’ essence in every dish, balancing light and shadow in flavours that felt both grounding and uplifting.

The Revival of Kagemori

Over the decades, Kagemori’s legacy endured. In 2020, Kenji revitalized the family tradition, reimagining the teahouse as a modern restaurant. Inspired by the original spirit of Kagemori, he infused the space with natural elements like lush greenery and soft lighting. In 2024, Kenji expanded Kagemori’s vision beyond Japan, opening its first international location in Selangor, Malaysia. With the help of business partners Lee Yee Kang and Lim Soon Jie, the Malaysian branch retains the essence of the original Kyoto restaurant while incorporating local influences, creating a harmonious blend of cultures that honours both tradition and innovation.

Our Promise

Today, Kagemori continues to honour its roots while embracing innovation, welcoming diners from all walks of life. Every detail—from the ambiance to the dishes—is designed to transport diners to a place of timeless serenity, keeping the heart of Kagemori alive for generations to come. We invite you to step into the shadows of Kagemori and experience the magic of a forest-inspired retreat, where every bite tells a story, and every moment is a memory in the making.
